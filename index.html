Climate financing made easier

Verdafin is a climate focused non-bank lender powering green companies and projects often overlooked by traditional banks. We fund innovative projects working towards a green, sustainable future, in areas such as renewable energy, energy efficiency and low-carbon technologies. We’re here to help India achieve its bold climate goals, combat climate change and build a sustainable future for the generations to come.

Powering possibilities for tomorrow

Climate change is the greatest challenge of our times, posing a threat to global wellbeing, economic stability and our future. Given India’s vulnerability to the impact of climate change and its admirable commitment to ambitious climate goals, the right financing, delivered swiftly, is the need of the hour.

The Government is taking steps to attract climate finance, but there is still a substantial funding gap. We’re helping fill this funding gap with our expertise, bespoke products and swift service, making India’s transition to a low-carbon economy easier and smoother.

Mr. Om Prakash Bhatt Chairman
